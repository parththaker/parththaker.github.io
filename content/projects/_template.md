## Problem

What was hard or unsolved? Why did it matter? Keep it concrete.

## Approach

What you actually did. Architecture, key decisions, trade-offs. A diagram or
code snippet here is high-signal.

## Result

Quantified outcome wherever possible (latency, cost, accuracy, adoption, time
saved). If the work is confidential, describe the shape of the result without
the numbers you can't share.

## Lessons

What you'd do differently / what you learned. This is what makes it a case
study instead of a brag.
