§ 01 The Author

Parth is an AI Research Engineer at Intuitive Surgical, where he builds secure, privacy-preserving LLM systems — retrieval assistants over internal documents and code, guardrail and security analysis for LLM applications, and LLM + CI/CD tooling.

He holds a Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering from Arizona State University (2024), advised by Prof. Gautam Dasarathy, with research spanning nonconvex optimization, graph theory, and multi-armed bandits. He earned his B.Tech + M.Tech at IIT Madras.

The throughline: rigorous theory that earns its keep in practice — from provable sample-complexity bounds to the guardrails that keep production language models honest. Read the full CV →
